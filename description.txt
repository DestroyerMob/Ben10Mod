Ben10Mod brings Ben 10 style transformation gameplay to Terraria.

Start with Heatblast, unlock more aliens through bosses and events, equip an Omnitrix in the custom Omnitrix slot, and use Plumber's Badges to fire each form's attacks.

Action keys can trigger timed abilities or load special badge attacks, and the current loaded attack is shown under the Omnitrix energy bar.

This mod is still in active development. Art, balance, and later-progression content are still being expanded.
