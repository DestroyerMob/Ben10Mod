Ben10Mod brings the world of Ben 10 into Terraria with transformable alien forms, custom combat styles, boss-based unlocks, and a growing roster of heroes, ultimates, and abilities.

Blast enemies apart as Heatblast, tear through fights with Rath, unleash Gwen's magic, experiment with Kevin's Osmosian absorption, and unlock more powers as your playthrough progresses.

This mod is built around flashy transformations, distinct alien identities, and progression that makes every new unlock feel like a big moment.

More content, polish, and transformations are still on the way.
